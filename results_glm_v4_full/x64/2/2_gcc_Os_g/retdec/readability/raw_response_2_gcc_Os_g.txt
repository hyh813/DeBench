**Best Quality Aspect:** Function Naming (Score: 7/10)

**Most Needs Improvement:** Composite Type Recovery (Score: 1/10)
- The complete failure to reconstruct `struct` definitions turns high-level data manipulation into low-level pointer math, which is the single biggest barrier to understanding the code.

**Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:**
1.  **Implement Floating-Point Type Recovery**: Map ARM64 floating-point registers/operations back to C `float` and `double` types and standard operators (`+`, `*`) instead of `int128_t` and `__asm_` intrinsics.
