**Best Quality Aspect:** Composite Type Recovery (Score: 9/10)

**Most Needs Improvement:** Literal Clarity (Score: 3/10)

**Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:**
1. **Annotate Constant Propagation:** When a function call is optimized away to a constant, insert a comment indicating the original call (e.g., `/* Optimized from: cdecl_func(5, 10) */`).
