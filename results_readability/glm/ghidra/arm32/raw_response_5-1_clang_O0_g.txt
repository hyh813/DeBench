**Overall Readability Score:** 6.7/10

**Critical Defects:**
- Exception handling (`try/catch` blocks) is completely flattened into runtime calls (`__cxa_throw`), removing the high-level control flow structure.

**Best Quality Aspect:** Function Naming Quality (Score: 9/10)

**Most Needs Improvement:** Comment Quality & Presence (Score: 3/10)
- The output lacks semantic comments, leaving complex C++ mechanisms (like virtual inheritance adjustments or exception landing pads) unexplained, which significantly hampers understanding of the "why" behind the code.

**Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:**
1.  **Reconstruct High-Level Exception Handling**: Identify `__cxa_throw` and corresponding landing pads to reconstruct `try/catch` blocks instead of leaving them as raw runtime calls.
