**Overall Readability Score:** 4.6/10

**Critical Defects:**

**Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:**
1.  **Recover Varargs Idioms**: Detect the standard `va_list` initialization and access patterns (e.g., `va_start`, `va_arg`) on x64 and abstract them into high-level loop structures rather than exposing register save area manipulation.
