- **Constant folding loses traceability**: `call_macro_constants()` returns `64` directly instead of showing `param_macro_constants(2048)` call

### Best Quality Aspect:
- **Function Naming Quality (9/10)** - Function names perfectly preserved from source, maintaining semantic clarity and making code navigation intuitive

### Most Needs Improvement:
- **Comment Quality & Presence (2/10)** - Complete absence of documentation despite original having extensive comments explaining macro semantics, assembly behavior, and test expectations

### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:
1. **Recover and preserve source comments** from debug symbols (`-g` flag) when available, especially for complex patterns like macros and assembly
2. **Annotate constant-folded expressions** with original computation (e.g., `return 64; // BUFFER_COUNT * 4 = 16 * 4`)
3. **Flag semantic transformations** with warnings when assembly is replaced, macros are expanded, or conditional compilation branches are resolved
