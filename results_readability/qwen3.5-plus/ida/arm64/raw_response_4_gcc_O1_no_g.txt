- **Variable names are entirely generic**: `a1`, `a2`, `v0`, `v1` provide zero semantic information about purpose

- **Type precision severely degraded**: All integers become `__int64`/`_DWORD`, losing `int`, `long long`, `size_t` distinctions critical for understanding ABI behavior

### Best Quality Aspect:

### Most Needs Improvement:
- **Composite Type Recovery (2/10)** - Complete failure to recover struct definitions is the most severe issue, as it fundamentally obscures the calling convention testing purpose of this code.

### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:
1. **Recover struct/union types from stack access patterns** - Detect repeated offset access patterns (`[0]`, `[1]`, `[15]`) and propose struct definitions with field names based on usage context.
