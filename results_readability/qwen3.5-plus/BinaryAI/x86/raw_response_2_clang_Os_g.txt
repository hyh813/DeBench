### Most Needs Improvement

### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement
1. **Recover composite type definitions** - Analyze struct access patterns (`param_1[1]`, `param_1 + 0x14`) to reconstruct struct layouts with field names based on usage context.
