**6. Function Decomposition:** Score: 7/10  
Justification: Functions maintain reasonable granularity. Helper functions preserved (`double_value`, `triple_value`). No excessive inlining or fragmentation observed.

**7. Code Linearization:** Score: 7/10  
Justification: Most functions read top-to-bottom reasonably. `goto_forward` and `goto_backward` successfully recovered as conditionals/loops. Some do-while where original had for/while.

**8. Redundancy Elimination:** Score: 6/10
