### Best Quality Aspect:

### Most Needs Improvement:
- **Comment Quality & Presence (2/10)** - Zero meaningful comments recovered; decompiler only outputs warnings, not semantic documentation that would explain intent.

1. **Recover constant semantics**: Cross-reference magic numbers with common patterns and suggest named constant definitions (e.g., `0x400` → `#define MAX_SIZE 1024`)

3. **Replace pseudo-instructions**: Convert `LOCK()`/`UNLOCK()` to standard C11 atomics (`atomic_fetch_add`) or inline assembly comments for portability
