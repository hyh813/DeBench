**3. Literal Clarity:** Score: 3/10

**7. Code Linearization:** Score: 3/10

**8. Redundancy Elimination:** Score: 4/10  
Justification: Significant redundancy in struct definitions (repeated `struct_0` with different `padding_0` sizes). Multiple thunk functions for same virtual methods.
