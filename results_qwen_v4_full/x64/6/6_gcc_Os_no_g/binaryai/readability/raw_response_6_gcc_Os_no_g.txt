### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement

1. **Implement semantic variable naming**: Analyze usage patterns to infer names (e.g., variables passed to `strcpy` first param → `dst`, second → `src`)

3. **Recover named constants from usage**: Detect repeated magic numbers (`0x2a` = 42 appears 20+ times) and suggest `#define` constants with semantic names
