### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement

1. **Recover composite types from stack access patterns**: Detect sequential memory accesses with consistent offsets and reconstruct struct definitions instead of exploding into individual parameters

2. **Preserve call relationships over constant folding**: When functions are called with constant arguments, show the actual call rather than pre-computing results, preserving semantic transparency

3. **Add semantic annotations for optimization artifacts**: Mark constant-folded returns, inlined functions, and register-spilled variables with comments explaining the transformation from source
