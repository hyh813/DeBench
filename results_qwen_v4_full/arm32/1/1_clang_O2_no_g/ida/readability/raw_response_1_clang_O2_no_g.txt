- **Generic variable naming** - Parameters `a1, a2, a3` and temporaries `v1, v2, result` obscure semantic meaning compared to original `a, b, c, x, n, temp1`

- **No contract documentation** - Preconditions like `n > 0` for loops not documented; error conditions unexplained

### Most Needs Improvement:
