### Most Needs Improvement:

### Top 3 Recommendations for Decompiler Improvement:
1. **Implement semantic variable naming**: Analyze usage patterns to infer meaningful names (e.g., variables used in array indexing should be named `index`, variables holding allocation results should be named `buffer` or `data`)

3. **Recover C++ idioms explicitly**: Label vtable calls as "virtual dispatch", mark RTTI operations, and document exception handling flow with appropriate comments
