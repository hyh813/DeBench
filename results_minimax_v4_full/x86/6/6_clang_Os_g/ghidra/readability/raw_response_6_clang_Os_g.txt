**Overall Readability Score:** 5.2/10

**Critical Defects:**
- **Variable naming**: Generic names like `sVar1`, `local_30`, `iVar2` obscure semantic meaning throughout

- **Comment absence**: No semantic comments recovered from source; only decompilation artifacts

- **Contract loss**: All expected return values and test expectations from source comments are lost

**Best Quality Aspect:** Function Decomposition (8/10)

**Most Needs Improvement:** Lexical Clarity (4.25/10 avg)

4. **Add semantic comments**: When source had explanatory comments (like "期望: 8"), preserve or reconstruct them
5. **Explain magic numbers**: Flag constants like `0x2a` with comments suggesting they may be return codes or sizes
