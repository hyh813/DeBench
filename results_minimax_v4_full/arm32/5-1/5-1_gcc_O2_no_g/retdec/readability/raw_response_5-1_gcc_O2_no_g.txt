Justification: Magic numbers like `42` (thrown exception value), `100`, `200` appear without explanation. The value `21` in constructor test is used directly. Some strings are encoded in hex (`\xe6\xb5\x8b\xe8\xaf\x95`). Constants like `g19`, `g20` have no semantic meaning.
